ISMIR 2010 has concluded • 16 August 2010

Thank you all for your contribution to and participation in this event!

ISMIR 2010 was attended by 245 participants. Any post-conference updates will be posted on this website. Printed conference proceedings can be ordered through IGITUR, the digital publishing service of Utrecht University Library. The papers remain available through this website as well, and will be included soon in the ISMIR Cumulative Proceedings hosted by IRCAM.

See you in Miami, USA for ISMIR 2011!
