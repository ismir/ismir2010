news

Registration and tutorials • 16 April 2010

ISMIR 2010 registration has been opened. Registration services are kindly provided by Utrecht Summer Schools. Utrecht Summer Schools also provide student accommodation at a very reasonable price. An extra accommodation discount is offered to participants who attend both the USMIR summer school and ISMIR. See Information for participants.
The titles of the tutorials have been announced as well. See the program for further details.

Submission deadlines extended • 26 February 2010

The deadline for submission of papers, tutorials and STAR papers has been extended to Wednesday 24 March, 2010. As a consequence, other dates will shift as well. The notification date is 12 May; the deadline for camera-ready papers is 2 June. Due to the constraints of the schedule, no further extension of the submission deadline is possible.

If you’d like to know what Utrecht is like, please read Joel Weickgenant’s article in The New York Times.

ISMIR submission system online • 25 February 2010

Authors can now use the ISMIR 2010 online submission system to submit their papers. See Information for authors for further details.

New information online • 27 January 2010

Today, the Important Dates have been added to the Website. f(MIR) 2010 now has its own page, and links to Music-ir.org, SoSoMIR, f(MIR) 2010 and MIREX 2010 have been added to the ISMIR links page. The menu structure has been slightly altered.

ISMIR 2010 Paper Templates online • 23 January 2010

The submission page containing information for authors and the ISMIR 2010 paper templates is now available. A paper submission system will be online soon.

USMIR summer school • 11 December 2009

Information about the USMIR summer school has been added. This summer school will take place the week preceding ISMIR 2010 and starts at Monday 2 August 2010.

Call for Papers • 10 December 2009

The call for papers has been published. The paper submission deadline is Wednesday, 10 March 2010.

ISMIR 2010 website online • 25 August 2009

You are currently visiting the website of the 11th International Society for Music Information Retrieval Conference that will be held in Utrecht, the Netherlands, August 9th – 13th 2010.
