accommodation

ISMIR 2010 participants have two accommodation options:

  1. to book a student room (cost 175 € for a week). Student rooms are provided by Utrecht Summer School and can be booked through the ISMIR Online Registration Page. Please visit the Utrecht Summer School housing page for more information on student rooms. This option is no longer available.
  2. to book a room at a hotel in Utrecht. Hotel rooms need to be booked separately, though discounts will be provided.
